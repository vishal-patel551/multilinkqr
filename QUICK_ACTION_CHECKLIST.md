## üöÄ SPEED TIPS

**To Enhance Content Quickly:**

1. **Use AI as Starting Point** (but heavily edit)
   - Generate outline
   - Get stat suggestions
   - Create example scenarios
   - ALWAYS add personal insights and edit heavily

2. **Borrow Structure, Not Content**
   - Look at competitor posts for ideas
   - Use their structure as template
   - Write completely original content
   - Add your unique perspective

3. **Batch Similar Tasks**
   - Write all intros at once
   - Add all images in one session
   - Write all FAQs together

4. **Focus on Top Performers**
   - Enhance pages that already get traffic
   - Improve pages that rank well
   - Skip low-performing pages initially
