About MultiLinkQR

Whether you're a small business owner, freelancer, content creator, or event organizer, MultiLinkQR empowers you to connect your offline and online worlds seamlessly.

What Makes MultiLinkQR Different

Unlike many QR code generators that require accounts, store your data in databases, or limit functionality behind paywalls, MultiLinkQR operates on a fundamentally different principle: your data stays with you.

  • No Account Required: Start generating QR codes immediately without creating an account or providing personal information.
  • Privacy-First Design: All your links and settings are encoded directly into the generated URL. We don't store your data in any database, which means your information never leaves your browser until you share the QR code.
  • Dual QR Code Support: Create both single-link QR codes for direct redirects and multi-link QR codes that open multiple destinations or display a landing page with organized buttons.
  • Completely Free: All features are available at no cost, with no hidden fees, premium tiers, or usage limits.
  • Client-Side Processing: The tool runs entirely in your browser using modern web technologies (HTML, Bootstrap, and JavaScript), ensuring fast performance and complete control over your data.

Key Features

Single-Link QR Codes

Perfect for simple use cases like linking to a single website, social media profile, or landing page. When scanned, the QR code directly redirects users to your specified HTTPS link.

Multi-Link QR Codes

Combine multiple links into one QR code. Users can either have all links automatically open in new tabs, or see a clean landing page with labeled buttons for each destination.

Customization Options

Customize your QR code with various colors, backgrounds, rounded pixel styles, and optional logos. Choose from preset colors or use custom hex codes to match your brand.

Expiry Dates

Set expiration dates for time-limited campaigns, promotions, or events. Once the expiry date passes, the QR code will no longer function, helping you maintain control over temporary marketing materials.

Multiple Export Formats

Download your QR codes as high-resolution PNG files for printing or as scalable SVG files for web use. Choose from small, medium, or large sizes depending on your needs.

UTM Parameter Support

Add UTM tracking parameters to your links for marketing campaigns. Track traffic sources, mediums, and campaigns through your analytics platform.

How It Works

MultiLinkQR uses a client-side architecture that processes everything in your browser:

  1. Input Your Links: Add one or more HTTPS links with optional labels (for multi-link QR codes) or a single link (for single-link QR codes).
  2. Customize Design: Choose colors, backgrounds, styles, and optionally upload a logo to personalize your QR code.
  3. Configure Settings: Set an expiry date if needed, choose between auto-open or button display modes, and add UTM parameters for tracking.
  4. Generate & Encode: All your settings and links are encoded into a base64 string and embedded in the URL. No data is sent to our servers.
  5. Download & Share: Download your QR code as PNG or SVG, or share the generated URL directly. The QR code works immediately and doesn't require any backend infrastructure.

This architecture ensures that your data remains private, the service remains fast and reliable, and you maintain complete control over your QR codes.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Privacy is at the core of MultiLinkQR's design. We don't require accounts, don't store your links in databases, and don't track your QR code usage. The only data we may collect is standard web analytics (like page views and device types) to help us improve the service, and this is done in an aggregated, anonymized manner.

When you create a QR code, all information is processed locally in your browser. The generated URL contains your data in an encoded format, but this is only accessible to people who have the QR code or the URL itself. We never have access to your links or settings unless you explicitly share the generated URL with us for support purposes.
