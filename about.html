About MultiLinkQR

MultiLinkQR is a lightweight, privacy-friendly multi-link QR code generator. It was built with a simple idea: make it easy for anyone to share multiple links from a single QR code without needing accounts, logins, or complex tools.

The tool runs entirely in your browser using HTML, Bootstrap and JavaScript. Your links are encoded directly into the URL, so we do not store your data in any database. You remain fully in control of the QR code and the landing page that your visitors see.

Whether you are a small business owner, freelancer, content creator or event organizer, MultiLinkQR helps you share your digital presence in a fast and professional way.
