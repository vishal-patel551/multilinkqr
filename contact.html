Email: support@multilinkqr.com

We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.

Best subject lines to use

  • Bug report: “Bug – QR not loading correctly on [device / browser]”
  • Feature request: “Feature request – [short description]”
  • Partnership: “Partnership – MultiLinkQR + [your brand / product]”
  • General question: “Question about multi-link QR codes”

Including screenshots, links and a short description of what you were trying to do helps us solve your issue faster.

What we can help with

  • Issues generating or scanning your QR code
  • Styling questions (colors, backgrounds, layout)
  • Multi-link landing page behavior and expiry
  • Use cases for business cards, menus, events and social media
  • API, integration or white-label discussions
  • Technical troubleshooting and browser compatibility
  • Best practices for QR code design and implementation
  • Questions about privacy, data handling, and security
  • Feature suggestions and product feedback
  • Partnership and collaboration opportunities

Response times

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 1–2 business days. For urgent technical issues that prevent you from using the service, please include "URGENT" in your subject line and we'll prioritize your request.

Note: We typically respond faster to bug reports and technical issues than to general questions or feature requests, as these directly impact user experience.

What to include in your message

To help us assist you more effectively, please include:

  • For bug reports: Browser name and version, device type (desktop/mobile), operating system, steps to reproduce the issue, screenshots or screen recordings if possible, and the generated QR code URL (if applicable)
  • For feature requests: A clear description of the feature, why it would be useful, and potential use cases
  • For general questions: Your specific question and any relevant context about how you're trying to use MultiLinkQR
  • For partnerships: Information about your company, product, or service, and how you envision collaborating with MultiLinkQR
