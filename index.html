What is a Multi-Link QR Code?

A multi-link QR code is a special type of QR code that contains more than one URL. When someone scans the QR code, they are taken to a landing page that can automatically open multiple browser tabs or display buttons for each link. This is ideal for business owners, creators and freelancers who want to share multiple destinations in one place.

With MultiLinkQR you can combine links to your website, online store, WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook page, LinkedIn profile, portfolio, YouTube channel and more. Instead of printing several QR codes on your visiting card or marketing material, you only need one clean, professional QR.

How to generate a multi-link QR code

  1. Click on the “Generate Multi-Link QR” button above.
  2. Enter a name for your QR code (for example "Ravi's Business Links").
  3. Add as many HTTPS links as you need and choose labels for each.
  4. Pick your preferred color, background and rounded style.
  5. Optionally set an expiry date for time-limited campaigns.
  6. Choose whether links should auto-open or be shown as buttons.
  7. Generate your QR code and download it as PNG or SVG.
  8. Print it on visiting cards, flyers, posters, or share the link directly online.
