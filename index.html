What is a multi-link QR code?

A multi-link QR code (also called a multi-URL QR or multi-destination QR) is a QR code that contains more than one link. When someone scans the code, they are taken to a landing page that can:

  • Automatically open all of your HTTPS links in new tabs, or
  • Show buttons for each link so users can tap what they need.

With MultiLinkQR, you can combine links to your website, online store, WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook page, LinkedIn profile, portfolio, YouTube channel and more. Instead of printing several QR codes on your business card or marketing material, you only need one clean, professional QR code. Our free QR code generator also supports single-link QR codes for direct redirects, making it perfect for simple use cases like linking to a single website or social media profile.

How to generate a multi-link QR code
