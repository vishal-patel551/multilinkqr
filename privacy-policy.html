Privacy Policy

MultiLinkQR is designed to be privacy-friendly. We do not require you to create an account and we do not store your links in a central database. When you create a QR code, your data is encoded inside the generated URL itself and processed in your browser.

We may use standard web server logs or analytics tools to understand how the website is used (for example total page views, device types and country-level statistics). This information is used only to improve the service.

If you embed third-party links (such as YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn or any external website), their own privacy policies will apply when visitors click those links. We recommend reviewing the privacy practices of any services you link to.
